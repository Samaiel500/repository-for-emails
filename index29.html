BKC Communicative English Teaching Course (BKC CET)
3 jULY - 28 jULY 2023
31 jULY - 25 AUGUST 2023
The BKC Communicative English Teaching (CET) course will give you a practical understanding of the principles, ideas, and more importantly the techniques of modern communicative language teaching.
This is a method of teaching which aims to put the student first, to involve them and motivate them as much as possible. We always remember that students do not come to our classrooms to learn about English, they come to learn how to use English effectively in a variety of different ways, different situations and for different purposes.
You will be assessed by experienced teacher trainers, who are also current, verified CELTA tutors.
Basically, a certificate from such a course shows the rest of the teaching community that you can teach, not just talk about teaching.
Course dates:
3 July - 28 July 2023 (Offline)
Days: Monday-Friday
Time: 12:00 - 21:00 (with a 60-min lunch break)
Course length: 4 weeks (120 ac.h.)
Course fee: 80 000 rubles
Course fee: 70 000 rubles (if paid by 16 June 2023)
2 places left!
